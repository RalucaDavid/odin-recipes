Tuna Salad

Description

Nothing is better and healthier than a tuna salad. This salad combines the taste of seafood with the taste of fresh vegetables.

It is very easy to prepare and it does not take much time to prepare it.

Ingredients

  • a can of tuna
  • mayonnaise
  • celery
  • red onion
  • lemon juice
  • parsley
  • garlic
  • a pinch of salt
  • a pinch of pepper

Steps

  1. Step one:

    Chop the vegetables and remove the tuna from the can.

  2. Step two:

    Add tuna and vegetables to a bowl and mix them together with the other ingredients.

  3. Step three:

    Enjoy the salad along with something from the bakery!
