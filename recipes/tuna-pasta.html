Tuna Pasta

Description

Do you want to feel the taste of Italian pasta and seafood at the same time? The answer is this recipe.

All you have to do is the delicious sauce of this recipe that can be used with any type of pasta

Ingredients

  • For the sauce

    • tomato sauce
    • basil
    • a pinch of salt
    • a pinch of pepper
    • a can of tuna
    • onion

  • For the pasta

    • spaghetti/other type of pasta
    • a spoonful of oil
    • a pinch of salt
    • 500 ml. of water

Steps

  1. Step one:

    Put water with salt and oil in a pot to boil. Chop the onion and put it with a little water in an already heated pan.

  2. Step two:

    When the water is boiling, put the pasta in the pot. When the onion is ready, take the tuna out of the can and put it in the pan. When the tuna is ready, put the tomato sauce with the spices in the pan and mix. Later add a little pasta water and mix.

  3. Step three:

    When the pasta and the sauce are ready, mix them together in the pan.

  4. Step four:

    Enjoy the tuna pasta!
