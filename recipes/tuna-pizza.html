Tuna Pizza

Description

One of the most popular pizza recipes is that of Tuna Pizza. It combines the Italian taste of pizza with the taste of seafood.

All we have to do is make the dough and prepare the toppings.

Ingredients

  • For the dough

    • 500 grams of flour
    • 300 ml of water
    • a spoonful of oil
    • 15 grams of yeast
    • a pinch of salt
    • a pinch of sugar

  • Toppings

    • tomato sauce
    • mozzarella
    • a can of tuna
    • black olives
    • red onion
    • basil

Steps

  1. Step one:

    In a bowl combine flour with salt, sugar and yeast. After that add water and oil. Mix them until the dough becomes non-sticky. Leave to to ferment in the refrigerator for a few hours.

  2. Step two:

    Until the dough is ready, prepare the toppings. Remove the tuna from the can, slice the olives, slices the onion and the mozzarella.

  3. Step three:

    When the dough is ready, put it in a tay and put the toppings over dough. Put the tay in the oven for 40 minutes at 200 Celsius degrees.

  4. Step four:

    Enjoy the pizza!
